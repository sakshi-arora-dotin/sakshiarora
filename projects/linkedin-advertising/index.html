LinkedIn Advertising Strategy

Creating a full-funnel content experience to boost leads and reduce costs on LinkedIn

Our calendar software company was founded with the goal of helping people better manage their time and stay organized. We recognized that many people struggle to keep track of their busy schedules, and we wanted to provide a simple and effective solution. With our calendar software, we've created an easy-to-use tool that allows users to quickly add and view events, set reminders, and invite others to events. We've also made sure to integrate seamlessly with popular email services, so that users can easily stay connected with their colleagues and friends.

Join us on our mission

Through awareness, education, and direct action, we're working to combat climate change and protect our precious ecosystems. Together, we can make a difference.

Since our founding, we've been at the forefront of environmental conservation. Our dedicated team of scientists, activists, and volunteers works tirelessly to protect endangered species, restore critical habitats, and educate the public about the urgency of climate action.

Target Audience

Marketing leaders at B2B SaaS companies in North America, Europe and Asia-Pacific.

Goals

To meet or exceed Marketing Qualified Lead (MQL) and pipeline targets for each quarter and year across all segments and markets.

Execution

  • Managed global LinkedIn campaigns with an annual budget exceeding $700,000 on LinkedIn to create a full-funnel content experience.

  • The top of the funnel audience included the broader software audience with contact-based ICP filtering. The middle of the funnel included audiences who engaged with the awareness campaigns, along with other retargeting audiences such as website visitors, event attendees, folks who've engaged with G2's content in the past, and folks who've visited G2's category pages.

  • The content spoke to demand generation, digital marketing leaders, and customer marketers and varied based on the different audiences at different stages of the funnel.

  • Tested different ad formats such as single image, though leadership and video ads on LinkedIn.
